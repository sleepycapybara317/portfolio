A Director's Portfolio

Overview

Through this project, I designed and developed a portfolio website for Raeya Ponugoti, an Atlanta-based director.

Film at the Focus

Instead of a hero image, I placed her film reel at the start of her website. This allows users to immediately grasp Raeya's medium of art and her talent.

Conclusion

Although it was tricky to design for an artist, this project was extremely rewarding. It not only allowed me to better understand how to stay true to someone else's vision, but it allowed me to make an impact by amplifying the local director's reach.
